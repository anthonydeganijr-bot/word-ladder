Word Ladder — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

Word Ladder is a small, free game with no account system and no sign-up. This page explains the little bit of data it does touch.

What's stored on your device

  • Your puzzle progress, streak, and whether you've seen the "How to Play" popup — stored locally in your browser (localStorage), never sent anywhere.

Daily reminder notifications (optional)

If you tap "Get a daily reminder," your browser creates a push subscription (an endpoint URL and encryption keys, no personal information) which is sent to and stored by a small backend so a daily reminder can be sent to it. You can turn this off anytime with the same button, which deletes the stored subscription. No account or identity is attached to it.

Analytics

This site uses Cloudflare Web Analytics to see aggregate visit counts. It's cookieless and doesn't track you individually across sites.

Advertising

This site may show ads served by Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies and similar technology to serve ads based on your visits to this and other sites. You can learn more and manage your ad personalization settings at Google Ads Settings, and read Google's own policy at policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

Children's privacy

This site isn't directed at children under 13 and doesn't knowingly collect information from them.
